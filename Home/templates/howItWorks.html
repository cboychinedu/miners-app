Your 3-Step Income Cycle

Getting started with MinerHub is simple and secure. Follow these steps to begin earning guaranteed returns.

1

Secure Deposit (NGN 4,500 Min)

Fund your account using our integrated Flutterwave payment gateway. A minimum deposit of NGN 4,500 is required before you can purchase a miner or post a link. The maximum deposit limit is NGN 2,000,000.

*All transactions are processed securely via Flutterwave.

2

Choose & Activate Your Miner

Browse the Miners page and select the PRO tier that fits your investment goal. Miner tiers start at NGN 4,500 and go up to NGN 28,500 (or custom tiers up to 2,000,000). Once purchased, the miner's cycle begins immediately.

3

Fixed Cycle Returns

Each miner tier has a fixed income and cycle duration (e.g., PRO I: NGN 9,000 income over 20 days). Once the cycle is complete, the total income is credited to your balance and the miner ceases operation. You can then choose to withdraw your funds or repurchase a new miner.

*You have full access and control over your account and login credentials.
